Problems

One problem I had was having the pseudocoding that I did in the index.html showing up randomly at the bottom of the webpage. I thought that adding the "//" meant that it was just a comment and it wouldn't show up. I realized after that the "//" only applied in the js and so I moved that pseudocoding into the results section instead.

Reflection

While doing this assignment I was a bit confused on how to make the game of rock, paper, scissors into a computer program. I thought hard about the steps of the game and even though its a simple game I was struggling with realizing it in this new form. I put in a lot of effort into this assignment, working on making this webpage look nice because my other labs are kinda ugly looking.
